About the Role

OHLMEC is seeking experienced Mechanical Fitters and Boilermakers to join our rapidly growing Kalgoorlie operations. We offer a dynamic work environment where safety, quality, culture, and integrity are our core values.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform maintenance and fabrication tasks on-site and off-site.
  • Ensure safety and quality standards are met across all projects.
  • Work in a fast-paced environment, adapting to dynamic project needs.

Requirements

  • Australian Recognised Trade Qualification.
  • Valid Australian Driver's Licence and Confined Space, Working at Heights certification.
  • Boilermakers need Gas Test Atmospheres certification.

Benefits

  • Attractive remuneration packages.
  • Opportunities for career growth in a growing company.
  • Flexible roster options available.
